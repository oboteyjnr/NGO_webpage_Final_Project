About Us

Charity Global is a non-profit organization committed to improving lives through humanitarian action, advocacy, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide support and hope to the most vulnerable, championing the rights of the homeless, refugees, and oppressed communities worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where every individual lives with dignity, free from poverty, and with access to basic human rights and opportunities.
